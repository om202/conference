About

The International Conference on Databases in Networked Information Systems (DNIS - 2019) (earlier named asÂ International Workshop on Databases in Networked Information Systems) will be held on Data Science and Analytics during September 12-14, 2019 at National Institute of Technology Delhi, India. DNIS-2019 provides an international forum for researchers and industry practitioners to share their original research results, practical experiences and thoughts on data science from different perspectives including databases, analysis, machine learning, artificial intelligence, their applications, open issues and future research trends.

Call for paper

TDNIS-2019 invites original, technically sound, high-quality research papers proposing novel solutions addressing the problems related to Data Science and analytics. Major topics of interest to the conference include, but are not limited to:
  • Analytics as a Service
  • Classification and regression
  • parallel and distributed learning
  • semi and unsupervised learning, reinforcement learning
  • Data Mining, graph mining and Analytics
